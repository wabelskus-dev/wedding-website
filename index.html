FAQs

A few helpful notes.

How far is the walk?

There is about a 0.5-mile walk along the beach from the parking lot to the ceremony site.

Should we carpool?

Yes, please carpool when possible. Parking near Tunnels Beach can be limited. Use Hāʻena Beach Park Parking, Kuhio Hwy / HI-560, Hanalei, HI 96714.

What should I bring?

Sunglasses or water may be helpful for the morning beach ceremony. Bring a beach chair if you would like to sit for the ceremony.

What should I wear?

We encourage a tropical color palette. For men, nice shorts or pants are welcome. Beach-friendly shoes are recommended, and barefoot is totally okay for guests too. Bri and Liam will both be barefoot for the ceremony. Please avoid wearing white.
